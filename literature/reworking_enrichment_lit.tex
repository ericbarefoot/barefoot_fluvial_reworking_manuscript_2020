\begin{ann}{foreman_fluvial_2012}
This paper forms the motivations for our work in the Piceance Basin in Western Colorado.
In this work, isotopic indicators of climate demonstrate that the the Molina Member of the Wasatch Formation records the time interval of the Paleocene-Eocene Thermal Maximum.
The study then connects the sedimentological signature of the Molina Member to climate change during the PETM, disproving several alternative hypotheses.
\end{ann}
